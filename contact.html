§ Before you write

A few frequent questions.

If your question is here, feel free to skip the form entirely.

Are you looking for a PhD position?

Yes — actively. I'm applying for the 2026–27 doctoral cycle, focused on adversarial ML and applied cryptography. Happy to discuss supervisor fit, thesis direction, and timelines.

Do you review papers?

I sub-review for venues my advisors delegate to me; I'm not yet a PC member anywhere independently. Please route formal review requests through the venue chairs.

Can I cite an unpublished result of yours?

Please email first — some in-progress work has embargo conditions attached. I'll usually say yes, with a citation form.

Do you take on student mentees informally?

Yes, with honest capacity caveats. My reading group is the easiest on-ramp; for 1:1 mentorship, please write with a specific question rather than a general ask.

How do I verify a message is really from you?

Important correspondence will be PGP-signed with the fingerprint on this page. If a message claims to be from me but isn't signed, please verify via a channel you already trust.

"Write briefly, write precisely, and include a link whenever possible."
